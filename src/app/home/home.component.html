What is this?

Walkr is mobile game where you explore net planets with a spaceship that is running on 'walking energy'.

The gyroscope in your phone is generating this energy so the game will encourage you to take a walk.

The further you come in the game, the more money upgrades will cost. In the beginning you explore many planets, and a walk in the park boost the progress a lot, but it gets slower and slower when you are playing for while.

This website is dedicated to optimizing playing strategies, so that you will progress quite fast. All relevant information from Reddit and Walkr Wikia are brought together on this website, plus much more. Please email me if you find missing or erroneous information.

Why an REST API?

A REST API is a convenient way to retrieve data from a system. You can use this Walkr API to build your own apps or just try it on this website.
